Home and Contents Insurance

Covers both your property and personal belongings.

  • Fire cover
  • Up to 12 months' accommodation if your home is unfit to live in
  • Flood and storm cover
  • Burglary, theft or attempted theft
  • Replacement of locks
  • Spoilage of food
See all features

Home and Contents Insurance features

 
  • 1
    Summary of features

    Fire, including bushfire

    We'll cover you if your home or contents are lost or damaged due to fire, including a bushfire. Limitations apply to cover for bushfire for the first 48 hours of your policy.

    Cover loss or damage
  • 1

    Up to 12 months' accommodation

    If we determine your home is unfit to live in as a result of loss or damage, we'll cover the cost of temporary accommodation until your home has been repaired, for a maximum of 12 months. Costs of accommodation are for an equivalent to your home’s rentable value before the loss and capped at 10% of the sum insured.

    Up to 12 months
  • 1

    Flood and storm cover

    We'll cover you for loss or damage as a result of a flood. Limitations apply to cover for storm for the first 48 hours and flood for the first 72 hours of your policy.

    Cover loss or damage
  • 1

    Burglary, theft or attempted theft

    We'll cover you for loss or damage as a result of burglary, theft or attempted theft. Limitations apply for the first 48 hours of your policy.

    Cover loss or damage
  • 1

    Replacement of locks

    We may cover you for up to $1,000 for replacement of locks or changing of house key codes if your keys are lost or stolen.

    Up to $1,000

  • Spoilage of food

    We'll cover you for up to $500 if food is spoiled inside your fridge or freezer as a result of an unplanned power outage.

    Up to $500

  • Explosion

    We'll cover you for loss or damage as a result of an explosion.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Lightning

    We'll cover you for loss or damage as a result of lightning.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Earthquake or tsunami

    We'll cover you for loss or damage as a result of an earthquake or tsunami. Limitations apply to cover for earthquake or tsunami for the first 48 hours of your policy.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Storm and rainwater runoff

    We'll cover you for loss or damage as a result of storm or rainwater runoff. Limitations apply to cover for storm and rainwater runoff for the first 48 hours of your policy.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Bursting, leaking or discharging or overflowing water or other liquid

    We'll cover you for loss or damage caused by incidents such as a burst water main, or an overflowing washing machine or dishwasher.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Malicious damage, intentional damage or damage by vandals

    We’ll cover you for loss or damage caused by incidents of malicious or intentional damage and vandalism.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Riot or civil commotion

    We'll cover you for loss or damage as a result of a riot or civil commotion.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Impact

    We'll cover you for loss or damage caused by incidents such as a fallen tree or vehicle impact.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Damage by an animal

    We’ll cover you for loss or damage caused by an animal excluding vermin, insects, birds and termites or where the animal entered, or is kept at, the property with your consent or the consent of a person permanently living with you.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Automatic sum insured adjustment

    We automatically adjust the sum insured each year based on the estimated replacement cost (for example, to reflect increases in repair costs). You don't have to accept the revised sum insured and we'll happily change it on your request.

    Adjusted for your benefit annually

  • Breakage of glass and sanitary fixtures

    We cover repair or replacement of fixed glass such as windows, and glass in furniture, pictures, paintings, mirrors and aquariums if they're accidentally broken. We also cover repair or replacement of fixed sinks, basins, baths and toilets if they're accidentally broken.

    Cover if accidentally broken

  • Contents in the open air

    We’ll cover your contents if they’re damaged from an insured event while being left outside, or stolen (subject to limitations applying to common areas). This is capped at $3,000 for any one insured event.

    Cover damage or stolen up to $3,000

  • Contents while temporarily removed

    If your home is damaged by fire, we'll cover the contents you take and keep with you (within Australia) while your home is unfit to live in. This is capped at 10% of the sum insured.

    Cover contents up to 10% of sum insured

  • Credit card or ATM cover

    We’ll cover you for the theft of money using your ATM card or the misuse of credit cards if they're stolen anywhere in Australia, provided it's reported to your financial institution within 24 hours and not otherwise covered by your financial institution. Capped at $1,000.

    Cover theft or misuse up to $1,000

  • Legal costs

    With our prior agreement, we’ll cover you for the legal costs involved in defending civil court proceedings arising from an insured event.

    Cover legal costs

  • Loss of rent

    If your insured home is rented out to tenants, and it becomes unfit to live in due to an insured event, we'll cover your loss of rent until it can be lived in again, up to a maximum of 12 months.

    Up to 12 months

  • Motor burnout

    We’ll cover you for the replacement or repair of household electric motors such as fridge motors (excluding electronic equipment such as TVs, sound systems and computers) if they burn out or fuse, provided they're less than 10 years old.

    Cover for replacement or repair

  • Moving to a new address in Australia

    We'll cover your contents from the date you begin to move out of your old address and to your new address for up to a maximum of 14 days. We’ll also cover your contents if they're damaged by fire or vehicle accident while you're moving them to the new address.

    Cover contents

  • Removal of debris and rebuilding costs

    If your house is damaged, we can assist with the rebuilding costs, including surveys, design, legal work, demolition and removal of debris, plus any additional costs required to comply with current government or council laws. Capped at 10% of sum insured.

    Assist with rebuilding costs up to 10% of sum insured

  • Unfixed building materials

    We'll cover you for loss or damage to unfixed building materials and uninstalled home fittings caused by an insured event. Capped at $500.

    Cover loss or damage

  • Legal Liability

    We'll cover you for up to $20 million for all legal liability claims arising from an incident in the home (owned by you) that causes death or bodily injury, or loss or damage to other people's property. We will cover up to $20 million for your legal liability to pay compensation as a result of an accident within Australia and New Zealand, which causes death or bodily injury, or damage to other people's property. A total cap of $20 million applies to all legal liability cover. Various exclusions also apply.

    Up to $20 million

  • Specified items away from home

    Cover anywhere in the world

  • Specified items inside the home

    If you have valuable items that exceed the contents cover limits, such as antiques or paintings, you can add them to your contents insurance and we'll cover their replacement value for loss or damage as a result of an insured event in the home. Subject to cover limits.

    Cover for items that exceed the contents cover limits

All benefits are subject to terms, conditions and limits.

    Covers some great extras
