THE HUT CLUB

The Hut Club has been designed to bring in members of our community to our R.M William Home. The Name References our travelling workshop - connecting us to people around the world, and references our humble beginings in 1932 by Reginald Murray Williams in his shed at 5 Percy Street in Prospect SA.

By joining the R.M. Williams Hut Club, you get access to:

  • VIP events in store
  • Special "Hints from The Hut" on boot and polish care, and retaining their super comfortable wear
  • Access to stylish custom-made belts
  • Visits to see the Master Craftsman in action, exclusive access to pre-season launches and much more.

New customers will have the opportunity to create an account during checkout
